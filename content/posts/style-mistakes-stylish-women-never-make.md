Stylish women are not always wearing expensive clothes. More often, they avoid the small mistakes that make outfits look unfinished.

## 1. Wearing clothes that almost fit

Too tight, too loose, too long or too short all create visual noise. Fit is the foundation.

## 2. Ignoring proportions

A loose top with loose trousers can work, but it needs intention. Most outfits benefit from balance.

## 3. Over-accessorising

One strong accessory usually looks more modern than five competing details.

## 4. Keeping dated items out of guilt

Clothes you bought years ago do not deserve wardrobe space just because they were expensive.

## 5. Wearing every trend at once

Trends are best used selectively. One current item can update a classic outfit.

## 6. Forgetting the shoes

Shoes can make or break the entire look.

## 7. Wearing tired basics

Basics need replacing. A stretched white T-shirt or faded black knit weakens everything around it.

## 8. Choosing comfort with no shape

Comfort is essential, but shape still matters.

## 9. Matching too literally

Perfectly matched shoes, bag and jewellery can look stiff. Coordinated is better than identical.

## 10. Ignoring fabric quality

Thin, clingy or shiny fabric often looks less expensive than simple matte fabric.

## 11. Skipping a final mirror check

Look at the outfit as a whole before leaving. Remove one distracting element if needed.

## 12. Dressing for an outdated version of yourself

Your style should match your current life, body and taste.

## 13. Confusing effort with excess

Polished style is edited, not overloaded.
