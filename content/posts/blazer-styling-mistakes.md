A blazer is one of the most useful items in a wardrobe over 40. It can instantly make jeans look smarter, elevate simple basics and add structure to softer outfits.

The problem is that small styling mistakes can make a blazer look stiff, corporate or dated.

## 1. Choosing the Wrong Shoulder Fit

The shoulder line is the foundation of the blazer.

## 2. Wearing an Oversized Blazer With Everything

Oversized blazers can look chic, but they do not suit every outfit.

## 3. Ignoring Sleeve Length

Sleeves that are too long can make a blazer look borrowed.

## 4. Keeping Outdated Shapes

Some blazer cuts instantly date an outfit.

## 5. Buttoning It Incorrectly

Single-breasted blazers generally look best with the lower button undone.

## 6. Wearing Harsh Black When Another Colour Works Better

Many women over 40 find navy, charcoal or cream easier to wear.

## 7. Pairing It With the Wrong Jeans

The blazer should work with the shape of the denim rather than fight it.

## 8. Overloading the Neckline

Scarves, necklaces and lapels should not all compete.

## 9. Choosing Stiff Fabrics for Casual Outfits

Softer fabrics often bridge the gap between smart and casual more successfully.

## 10. Forgetting the Back View

Always check the silhouette from behind.

## What to Do Instead

Focus on fit, proportion and simplicity. A well-chosen blazer can make almost every outfit look more polished.
