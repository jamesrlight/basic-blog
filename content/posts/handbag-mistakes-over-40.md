The wrong handbag can make an outfit feel dated, cluttered or mismatched.

## Common mistakes

- Carrying a worn-out bag with visible damage
- Choosing a bag that is too small for everyday use
- Choosing a bag that is far too large for your frame
- Overly busy hardware and logos
- Ignoring proportion
- Matching every accessory perfectly
- Carrying occasion bags during the day
- Neglecting maintenance
- Prioritising trends over usefulness

The best handbags quietly support the outfit rather than dominate it.
