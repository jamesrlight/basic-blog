Shoes are not an afterthought. They change the line of the leg, the mood of the outfit and how modern the whole look feels.

## 1. Wearing heavy shoes with delicate outfits

Chunky shoes can look current, but they need balance. A very heavy shoe with a soft, light outfit can pull the whole look downward.

## 2. Choosing ankle straps that cut the leg

Ankle straps can be elegant, but thick or high-contrast straps may shorten the leg line. Nude, low-contrast or lower-cut styles are often easier.

## 3. Keeping dated round-toe pumps

Some round-toe pumps still work, but overly chunky versions can date an outfit quickly. A sleeker toe shape often looks more current.

## 4. Wearing flats with no structure

Very soft, shapeless flats can make outfits look tired. Look for some definition, sole structure or a refined shape.

## 5. Pairing cropped trousers with the wrong shoe

Cropped trousers need careful shoe choice. If the shoe is too bulky or cuts the ankle, the proportions can look off.

## 6. Ignoring shoe condition

Scuffed heels, worn soles and collapsed backs can ruin an otherwise polished outfit.

## 7. Choosing comfort shoes that look purely practical

Comfort matters. But there are now plenty of comfortable shoes with clean lines. Practical does not need to mean clumsy.

## What to do instead

Choose shoes that support the outfit’s shape. Look at the full-length mirror, not just the shoe itself.
